# Brief — E-commerce product detail page

**Type:** product detail page (PDP) · **Stack:** Astro

Build the product detail page for the **Aera 01**, a $240 pair of minimalist running shoes from a direct-to-consumer brand. The page must sell one well-made product: a strong gallery (4–5 angles + a detail shot), the name and price above the fold, a size selector with a clear add-to-bag, three reasons-to-believe backed by specifics (weight in grams, the materials, the return policy), an accordion of details (materials · care · shipping), and a slot for reviews with a real-looking average. Conversion clarity matters (one obvious add-to-bag, no decision noise) but the brand is design-led, so the page should feel considered, not like a stock Shopify theme. Photography stands in via tasteful placeholders with the right aspect ratios.

# Brief — Agency / studio home

**Type:** studio homepage (single page, scroll) · **Stack:** Astro

Build the homepage for **Format North**, a five-person brand studio that does naming, identity, and packaging for food and drink startups. The site is their portfolio *and* their pitch — it has to demonstrate taste in the first scroll, because that's the product they sell. It needs a stance-setting hero (a sharp positioning line, not a tagline platitude), a selected-work strip (4–6 projects with client + discipline + a representative image), a short "how we work" in three honest steps, a credibility band (named clients / a result), and a "Start a project" prompt. A studio site that looks like a generic agency template fails the brief on sight; commit to one bold, coherent art direction with a signature moment.

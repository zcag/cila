# Brief — Pricing page

**Type:** pricing page (single page) · **Stack:** Astro

Build the pricing page for **Plotline**, a writing tool for novelists ($0 / $12 mo / $20 mo team). The job: make the choice obvious and reduce the anxiety of committing. It needs a clear three-tier comparison (Free · Author · Studio) with one recommended tier called out honestly, a feature list that's scannable (not a 40-row matrix of checkmarks), a monthly/annual toggle, a short FAQ addressing the real objections (can I cancel, what happens to my drafts, is the free tier crippled), and a single confident CTA per tier. Pricing pages are where trust is won or lost — clarity, honesty, and a calm hierarchy beat persuasion tricks; no fake urgency, no dark patterns. Still must look designed, not like a default pricing component.

# Brief — Editorial / brand story page

**Type:** long-form editorial marketing page · **Stack:** Astro

Build a brand-story page for **Tideline**, a small-batch coffee roaster sourcing single-origin lots from coastal farms. This is not a shop page — it's the "why us" story that earns the wholesale inquiry. It should read like a well-set magazine feature: a strong opening spread, the origin narrative in 3–4 movements (the farms, the roast philosophy, the people, the standard), pull-quotes from growers, generous typographic rhythm, and one quiet but distinctive layout idea. Close with a low-pressure "Talk to us about wholesale" prompt. Editorial typography and pacing are the whole point; the kill-list applies hard (no "passionate," no "crafted with love").

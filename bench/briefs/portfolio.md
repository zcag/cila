# Brief — Personal portfolio

**Type:** portfolio (single page, scroll) · **Stack:** Astro

Build the portfolio home for **Mara Vey**, an independent motion designer who animates title sequences and brand idents for film and music clients. She wants the site itself to feel like one of her pieces — confident, cinematic, a little restless — without being a Webflow-template cliché. The page leads with her name and a one-line stance, then a curated reel of 5–6 selected works (each: title, client, year, role), a short "approach" note in her own voice, and a single contact prompt. No nav-heavy layout, no skill bars, no generic "Hi, I'm a passionate designer." One memorable signature moment is expected (and must be reduced-motion-safe).

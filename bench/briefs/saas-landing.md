# Brief — SaaS landing page

**Type:** marketing landing (single page) · **Stack:** Astro

Build the landing page for **Cohere Drift**, a tool that watches a team's design tokens across repos and flags drift the moment a component diverges from the system. Buyers are frontend platform leads at 50–500-person product companies who are tired of "design-system rot" — components that slowly stop matching Figma. The page must land one core message (system-conformance you can't fool), show the before/after of a drift catch, and drive a "Connect your repo" CTA. Free for one repo; paid per additional. No pricing table on this page — just the hero, the proof of a catch, three concrete capabilities, social proof slot, and the CTA.
